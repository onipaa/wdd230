PARC: Repetition

Repeat design elements (e.g., consistency in alignment and headings) throughout a text. Repeat words and phrases for emphasis and clarity.

PARC: Contrast

The idea of contrast is to make dis-similar elements very different. Contrast is one of the most powerful visual design tools.

Whitespace

Whitespace, as it it called is the eseential space between elements. Illustrators, architects, and artist have used white space to create tension and energy as well as provide balance and harmony between elements. It may not even be white at all. For example, in recent Star Wars movies the black, starlight space is often crowded by an ominus wedge in the beginning of each movie. As you see the wedge of a space ship fill the screen, you may begin to feel a sense that the empire is encroaching, crowding out the rebellion. Such is the effect of whitespace.
