PARC: Repetition

Repeat design elements (e.g., consistency in alignment and headings) throughout a text. Repeat words and phrases for emphasis and clarity.

PARC: Contrast

The idea of contrast is to make dis-similar elements very different. Contrast is one of the most powerful visual design tools.

Whitespace

Whitespace, as we call it, is the essential space between elements. Illustrators, architects, and artists have used white space to create tension, energy, balance, and harmony between elements. It may not even be white at all. For example, in recent Star Wars movies, the black starlight space is often crowded by an ominous wedge at the beginning of each film. As you see the wedge of a spaceship fill the screen, you may feel that the empire is encroaching, crowding out the rebellion. Such is the effect of whitespace.
