Pictou County Archery Club Shoots Straight Into Provincials

Sarah Jordan | Posted: June 22, 2023, 3:45 p.m. | Updated: June 22, 2023, 4:03 p.m.

What was supposed to be just a fun day of shooting turned into provincials, due to weather and the recent fire and woods bans postponing the originally scheduled event. NSSAC was founded in 2010 and has approximately 25 active members at the moment. They mainly focus on 3D shooting, however, due to the advantage of new venues to shoot at, they are starting to do more target shooting and field shooting.
