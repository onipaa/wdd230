New Glasgow is a lovely place. I spent about six months of my mission there. It has a lovely river that runs through the middle of the town, and a lot of high-end architecture around the river. I used many place holders from picsum because I couldn't find a whole bunch of quality pictures from New Glasgow. I spent my second stint in New Gasgow living in the neighborin city/suburb of Antigonish. That is where the college is, and the missionaries had run up the miles on the car by over two months. So, we walked nearly everywhere. My companion was super trunky. He didn't like how hard we worked (at all).
